Cyclist Penguin Histograms


I chose the cyclist penguin as my source of student data. Due to the larger amounts of data for homework and quiz grades, I decided to make histograms for both categories to display the frequency of the various grades ranges earned by the "student" in each section. I generated my graphs by using google docs to create a spreadsheet that would hold all of the needed data. From there, I used the chart functions of goole docs to make the two histograms from the data sets I chose. I chose to have two spearate histograms due to the separate sections having different max scales. I also did not include the exam scores simmply based on the lack of data entries.
